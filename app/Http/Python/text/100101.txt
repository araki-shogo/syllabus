Outline

The overall aim of Uniﬁed English is to help students develop all 
four language skills (listening, speaking, reading, and writing) in 
English and to become eﬀective learners and communicators. To 
achieve this goal, students will engage in interactive language 
activities with their classmates and reﬂect on their learning both 
orally and in writing.
