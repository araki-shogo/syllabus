概要

到達目標

The overall aim of Unified English is to help students develop all four language skills (listening, sp
eaking, reading, and writing) in English and to become effective learners and communicators. To
achieve this goal, students will engage in interactive language activities with their classmates an
d reflect on their learning both orally and in writing. 
　

At the end of this course, students should be able to speak in English using everyday vocabulary
. Students should be able to listen to, read, and understand short passages in English and should
be able to write short passages in English with correct grammar. Students should also be able to
interact positivively with their classmates in pairs or in groups.��
