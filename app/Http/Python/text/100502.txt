eal situations (e.g. restaurants, shops, travel, parties, etc.). We can develop our ideas and build r
elationships by increasing opportunities to use new phrases and vocabulary.　

Speaking will be the key to this course but you will also be able to read a variety of texts and try
discussing them. At the end of the course you should be able to read a text without too much tra
nslating and speak about the things you have read.

実践的スキル

This course should help to build on the phrases and speech patterns you know. Pronunciation 
practice and grammatical improvements will help your conﬁdence. We will try to set up some s
cenarios in which you might ﬁnd yourself speaking English. For example, in the work place, ch
atting with foreigners, talking with oﬃcials, dealing with problems abroad. Also you should be 
ready to help your classmates to improve. If you think you can help them, please try to motiva
te each other. Also your ideas on what you want to study will be listened to, I promise! We will
follow a course book, but you will need something to keep all extra handouts materials. (Go to 
a 100 yen shop and buy a folder). Also, bring a dictionary.
