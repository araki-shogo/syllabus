概要

到達目標

The ﬁrst year course will focus on the development of speaking and listening skills.  The theme 
of this course is travel.  The core of this course is based on common phrases and situations most
likely encountered.  Using this core as a basis, students will be given the opportunity to expand t
heir communication skills.　

By the end of the course, students will be able to communicate eﬀectively in diﬀerent travel situ
ations.  Students will ultimately gain awareness that English is a tool for communcating with peo
ple from diﬀerent countries around the world.
