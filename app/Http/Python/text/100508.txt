概要

到達目標

This course will help students build conﬁdence in speaking English. Now is the time to skill up a
nd communicate with your friends and people around the world. We will study real English for r
eal situations (e.g. restaurants, shops, travel, parties, etc.). We can develop our ideas and build r
elationships by increasing opportunities to use new phrases and vocabulary.
　

At the end of this course, students should be able to speak in English using everyday vocabulary
.  Students should be able to listen to, read, and understand short passages in English. Students 
should also be able to interact positivively with their classmates in pairs or in groups.
