Outline

This course will help students build conﬁdence in speaking English. 
Now is the time to skill up and communicate with your friends and 
people around the world. We will study real English for real 
situations (e.g. restaurants, shops, travel, parties, etc.). We can 
develop our ideas and build relationships by increasing opportunities 
to use new phrases and vocabulary.
