概要

到達目標

This course will help students build confidence in speaking English. Now is the time to skill up an
d communicate with your friends and people around the world. We will study real English for real 
situations (e.g. restaurants, shops, travel, parties, etc.). We can develop our ideas and build rela
tionships by increasing opportunities to use new phrases and vocabulary.
　

At the end of this course, students should be able to speak in English using everyday vocabulary
.  Students should be able to listen to, read, and understand short passages in English. Students 
should also be able to interact positivively with their classmates in pairs or in groups.�
