概要

到達目標

Global awareness is a must for every global citizen. In this class we will be investigating some of
the major global issues which are important to the world now and will become more important i
n the years to come. The issues covered will be: Climate Change, Energy Resources, Food Suppl
y, Globalization, Habitat Preservation, Health, Human Rights, Migration, Pollution, Population G
rowth, Standard of Living, and Water Resources.　

This course will endeavor to acquaint the participants with many of the global issues facing our 
world now and in the future. The approach will be to understand what the issues are, what caus
es them and what measures are being attempted to alleviate them.

Outline

The overall aim of Uniﬁed English is to help you develop all the four language skills
(listening, speaking, reading, and writing) in English and become eﬀective learners
and communicators. To achieve this goal, you will engage in interactive language
activities with your classmates and reﬂect on your learning both orally and in writing.
