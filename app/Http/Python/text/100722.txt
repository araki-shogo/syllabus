概要

到達目標

In this class, students will improve their English skills through engaging in a variety of topics rel
evant to current society.  In addition, students are encouraged to express their opinions and be 
open to other points of view.　

This course will improve listening and speaking skills from intermediate level onward.  The goal 
of the class is to have you raise these skills within your ability. This class can help you, but you 
must be willing to work hard not only in class by practicing, doing quizzes and preparing for pre
sentations, but also by practicing outside of class as well.

実践的スキル

This class will use a textbook that will give you a lot of practice in listening and reading, and p
ractice in dictation. Each week you will: 1. Do homework 2. Do textbook activities 3. Practice 
vocabulary and speaking as a whole class and in pairs.  In addition you will: 1. Work on activit
ies that demand a mix of creativity and thinking. 2.  Using various other media resources, indi
viduals will be encouraged to voice opinion and discuss.
