実践的スキル

Using the textbook, students will do a combination of individual work, pair work and group wo
rk, with a heavy emphasis on pair work. Whilst discussing the issues, students will improve the
ir skills in listening, reading, writing and speaking. There will be many tasks in which pairs will
present their opinions to each other. There will also be a ﬁnal PowerPoint presentation.
