In this class, students will improve their English skills through engaging in a variety of topics rel

This course will improve listening and speaking skills from intermediate level.  The goal of the cl
ass is to have you raise these skills within your ability. This class can help you, but you must be 
willing to work hard not only in class by practicing, doing quizzes and preparing for presentatio
ns, but also by practicing outside of class as well.
