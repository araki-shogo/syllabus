到達目標

Global awareness is a must for every global citizen. In this class we will be investigating some of 
the major global issues which are important to the world now and will become more important in
the years to come. The issues covered will be: Climate Change, Energy Resources, Food Supply, 
Globalization, Habitat Preservation, Health, Human Rights, Migration, Pollution, Population Growt
h, Standard of Living, and Water Resources.�

This course will endeavor to acquaint the participants with many of the global issues facing our 
world now and in the future. The approach will be to understand what the issues are, what cause
s them and what measures are being attempted to alleviate them. �
