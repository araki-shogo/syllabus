概要

到達目標

The overall aim of this class is to help students continue to develop and improve on all four lang
uage skills (listening, speaking, reading, and writing) and to become eﬀective learners and comm
unicators in English. To achieve this goal, students will engage in interactive language activities 
with their classmates and reﬂect on their learning both orally and in writing. 　

At the end of this course, students should be able to speak in English using everyday vocabulary
. Students should be able to listen to, read, and understand medium-length passages in English a
nd should be able to write medium-length passages in English with correct grammar. Students sh
ould also be able to interact positivively with their classmates in pairs or in groups.

実践的スキル

Using the textbook, students will do a combination of individual work, pair work, and group w
ork, with a heavy emphasis on pair work. There will be many tasks in which pairs will work to
gether to answer questions, interview each other, or make conversations. There will be a ﬁnal t
est on speaking, vocabulary, grammar, reading, and listening.
