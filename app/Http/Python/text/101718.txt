Outline

The overall aim of this class is to help students continue to develop and improve on all four lang
uage skills (listening, speaking, reading, and writing) and to become eﬀective learners and comm
unicators in English. To achieve this goal, students will engage in interactive language activities 
with their classmates and reﬂect on their learning both orally and in writing.
