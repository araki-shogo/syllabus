This course provides practical skill questions for the TOEIC Test to help students get a higher T
OEIC score. There are both listening and reading sections with vocabulary.  We will focus on all 
the 
diﬀerent types of questions used in TOEIC. And, as much as possible, we will follow the same ti
me constraints, time pressure. We will do a lot of reading, vocabulary building, and listening.
　

Your TOEIC score is important, and the goal of the class is to have you raise it within your abilit
y. This class can help you, but you must willing to work hard not only in class by practicing and 
doing reading quizzes and tests, but also by practicing outside of class as well.
