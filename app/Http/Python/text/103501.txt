到達目標

This course will help you to tell the world about Japan and how you live your life in this exciting
place. From festivals to manga, from school life to work, we will cover all aspects of what is cool
and uncool about Japan. If you are asked about what Japan is like, this course will give you a ch
ance to talk about your experiences.　

You will be able express your thoughts and feelings on a number of topics related to your own e
xperiences. You will be able to ask others about what they feel about their life in English. Also y
ou will have a chance to challenge each other on their beliefs. (Donʼt worry  we can create a pre
tend debate for this part).
