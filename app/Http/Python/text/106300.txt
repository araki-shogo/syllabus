ocations that students need to communicate actively within a wide range of relevant and intere

three class periods.The whole aim of this course is to maximize student talking time. You will be 
expected to speak as much as possible in a fun, active learning environment. The material and re
sources will help you to become better speakers, and you will help each other. There will be a sp
eaking project at the end of every unit.�

Students will improve their speaking skills through a supporting framework for learning that prov

idence. There will be a high degree of peer cooperation, learning, and teaching, ensuring student
