概要

到達目標

Academic Writing I (A) will help students to develop and organize their ideas in English. The les
sons will help them understand how to write 2 diﬀerent English essay patterns. They will also le
arn how to correctly use outside sources in their writing to avoid plagiarism.　

Students will gain basic knowledge and skills for academic writing. Students will become familia
r with the notion of plagiarism and how to use outside sources to support their ideas using APA 
style. Students will become familiar with peer review  how to give and receive useful feedback. 
They will also review pertinent grammar points and mechanics for writing through homework as
signments.

実践的スキル

○　

This class will be conducted in workshop" style. Students will be either 1) writing  2) discussing
his/her writing with a classmate 3) discussing his/her writing with the teacher or 4) learning an
important topic related to writing. The teacher will provide supplemental materials on a partic
ular writing topic (i.e. topic sentence explanation and practice) almost every lesson.  The langu
age of instruction is English. (授業は英語で行う)
