到達目標

This is an English class to practice and improve your academic writing skills. You will learn thre
e diﬀerent modes of academic writing in order to become a better writer. Each mode covers two
units: ﬂuency and accuracy. You will also learn how to correctly use outside sources to avoid pla
giarism. There will be in-class writing, peer review and presentation. 　

The goal of this class is to improve your academic writing skills. You will learn about the writing
process, how to organize and format diﬀerent types of writing, and how to edit them properly. Y
ou will also become familiar with the notion of plagiarism and how to use outside sources to sup
port your ideas using APA style. Additionally, you will become familiar with peer review.
