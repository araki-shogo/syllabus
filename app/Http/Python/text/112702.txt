この講義は、英語の語法や表現を理解しながら、正確な英作文を作成する訓練を行います。英文法も復
習しますが、文法の問題を解くのではなく、これまでに学習してきた英語の知識を応用し、自分の考
えを英語で表現します。前期はsentence levelに特化し、より高度な文章訓練とパラグラフライティン
グを学習します。

Outline

This class will help students to develop and organize their ideas in English.
This course deals with writing the essays of the speciﬁc topics for proper sentences. The purpose
of this course is to develop English writing skills and write English sentences accurately. Student
s will review some grammatical issues to practice writing English sentences ﬁrst. Then, students 
will write an essay based on the course textbook. After writing the paper,  they are required to 
make a presentation in English.
