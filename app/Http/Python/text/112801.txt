units: fluency and accuracy. You will also learn how to correctly use outside sources to avoid pla
giarism. There will be inclass writing, peer review and presentation. �

The goal of this class is to improve your academic writing skills. You will learn about the writing p

port your ideas using APA style. Additionally, you will become familiar with peer review. �
