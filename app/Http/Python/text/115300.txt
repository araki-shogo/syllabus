到達目標

The English Language Institute (ELI) at Missouri State University is an intensive program oﬀering
stateoftheart education to international students to improve their academic language proﬁciency.
　

In every class, skills necessary to succeed at an American university are emphasized and practic
ed. Skills including research writing, critical reading, presenting, and acquiring knowledge from 
academic lectures are integral to our program.　The language of instruction is English.
