概要

到達目標

This course is intended to expand students' cultural awareness, cultural knowledge, and cultural 
skills for interacting with diverse groups of people. We will focus on enhancing knowledge of the
value systems and practices of diverse groups and factors related to age, gender, ability, and rac
e.
＊The language of instruction for this class is English.　

Students are expected to: 
① understand the core principles and practice of multiculturalism as well as its main criticism, 
② become familiar with values and assumptions across cultures, 
③ explore diﬀerent ways of categorizing the cultures, 
④ think critically and investigate generalizations made about other cultures.
