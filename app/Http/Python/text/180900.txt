概要

到達目標

Problem Based Training (PBT) is a new type of study. It aims to improve students' creative thinki
ng and ability to solve practical problems to cope with challenges in areas of global business, cr
osscultural communication, local business development and international aﬀairs, etc. Students' t
ask is to identify problems in the society based on their independent thinking and discuss with c
lassmates to ﬁnd solutions to the problems.　

As a new type of study, PBT focuses on developing and improving students' ability to identify pr
oblems and ﬁnd solutions to the problems. After the course, students will have abundant experie
nce and skills of problem solving. Students will gain diﬀerent perspectives by exchanging views 
with classmates. Students will also acquire creative thinking ability and teamwork spirit. These a
re very useful qualities when they go to the society.

実践的スキル

 

Lecture, discussion, and presentation. This class will be conducted completely in English. The t
eacher will raise some example problems for students to discuss and ﬁnd solutions. Each stude
nt is also required to think about one social problem and share it with other students for discus
sion. Finally every student should summarize the solutions and make a presentation on the pro
blemsolving process.
