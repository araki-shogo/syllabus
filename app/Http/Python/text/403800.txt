(1) Definition and issue of "Region (society)" "Community development"
(2) Theories and methods aimed at "community development from below and within" (public lif
e studies, local studies, related population, slow city)
(3) We will examine these in a workshop format, especially paying attention to regional develop
ment of Maebashi city and Gunma.�
